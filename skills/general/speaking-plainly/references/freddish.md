# The Nine Steps of Freddish

Writers for *Mister Rogers' Neighborhood* used these steps to translate everyday English into "Freddish" to prevent confusing or scaring children.

## The Translation Steps
1. **State the idea clearly**: Use terms the audience can understand.
2. **Rephrase positively**: Describe what to do rather than what not to do.
3. **Redirect to trusted authorities**: Direct the reader to trusted people or resources.
4. **Eliminate directives**: Remove instructive or prescriptive wording.
5. **Remove absolute certainty**: Rephrase words that suggest absolute certainty.
6. **Ensure inclusivity**: Avoid assumptions that do not apply to all readers.
7. **Add simple motivation**: Give a clear reason to follow the advice.
8. **Repeat step 1**: Rephrase to avoid value judgments.
9. **Relate to development**: Connect the advice to growth or personal development.
